Discover Qatar in 144 Hours

  1. Home
  2. Travelogue
  3. Qatar

Being a member of Youth Hostel Association of India certainly has its perks. One of them being getting the opportunity to travel to all sorts of different places in or outside India. As soon as I received the pamphlet about the program "Discover Qatar in 144 Hours", my mother convinced me to enroll in it and that's what I did. The program was scheduled from 22nd September 2019 to 27th September 2019. It was organised by the Qatar Youth Hostels and we were the delegates from India that were going to visit. In this time we were shown countless places in Qatar. Some of them being :-

  • Qatar National Library
  • Sheikh Faisal Museum
  • Barzan Towers
  • Katara Cultural Village
  • Qatar National Museum
  • Al Janoub Stadium and many more...

I am so thankful to the Qatar Youth Hostels for this opportunity. Also I feel very lucky to have met Aman, Badonbok, Sameep, Nishad, Sindura, Ankita, Amrutha, Radhika and Anu. The five days that all of us had spent together can never be forgotten. It was so cool to witness something for the first time together. Hopefully more of such programs take place and these expeditions never end.
